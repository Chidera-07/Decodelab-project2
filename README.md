### Outlier Detection

Because the Total Price variable displayed positive skewness, both Z-Score and Modified Z-Score methods were used.

#### Z-Score Formula:

Z-Score = (X - Mean) / Standard Deviation

#### Excel Formula:

=(D2-$M$3)/$M$2

#### Modified Z-Score Formula:

Modified Z=0.6745*(X-Median)/(MAD)

#### Excel Formula:

=0.6745*((D2-$M$4)/MAD)

#### Outlier Classification:

=IF(OR(ABS([@[Z_Score]])>3,ABS([@[Modified Z-Score]])>3),"Extreme Outlier",
IF(OR(ABS([@[Z_Score]])>2,ABS([@[Modified Z-Score]])>2),"Potential Outlier",
IF(OR(ABS([@[Z_Score]])>1,ABS([@[Modified Z-Score]])>1),"Slightly Unusual","Normal")))

#### Classification Thresholds

| Threshold | Classification |
|------------|---------------|
| Score > ±1 | Slightly Unusual |
| Score > ±2 | Potential Outlier |
| Score > ±3 | Extreme Outlier |

#### Findings:

* The analysis identified several high-value transactions classified as potential and extreme outliers. 
* The observations were retained after review because they represented legitimate customer purchases rather than data-entry errors.

### Trend Analysis and Key Observations

Trend analysis was conducted using Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, histograms, and scatter plots to identify patterns within the dataset and uncover significant observations.

* Instagram generated strong revenue for Desk and Laptop sales.
* Email performed strongly across several product categories.
* Marketing channel effectiveness varied by product.

### Coupon Performance
#### Revenue generated by coupon usage showed that:

* FREESHIP produced the highest revenue.
* Customers continued purchasing even without discounts.
* Coupon performance remained relatively balanced across categories.

### Key Findings:

* Total Price exhibited positive skewness (0.89).
* Most variables displayed approximately normal distributions.
* High-value transactions contributed to a long right-tail distribution.
* Outliers identified were valid business transactions and were retained.
* FREESHIP generated the highest revenue among coupon categories.
* Email and Instagram were the strongest-performing referral channels.
* Revenue declined steadily from 2023 to 2025.
* Cancelled and Returned orders exceeded Delivered orders.
